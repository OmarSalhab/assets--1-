Amman Arab University

TEDx Amman Arab University is an independently organized event, licensed by TED, dedicated to spreading ideas worth sharing. It brings together inspiring voices, meaningful stories, and diverse perspectives from the university community and beyond, creating a platform for dialogue, creativity, and impact.

We believe that when ideas pass through our stage like light through a prism, their true colors are revealed and their impact becomes stronger.
